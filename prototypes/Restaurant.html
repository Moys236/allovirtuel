Our Story

Founded in 2015, AlloFood began as a small food truck with a big dream - to revolutionize the burger experience in Tangier. Our founder, Chef Omar, combined his culinary training in Paris with his love for Moroccan flavors to create something truly unique.

Today, we operate three locations across the city, but we've never lost sight of our roots. Every burger is still made with the same care and attention to detail that won us our first loyal customers.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, working with trusted farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our beef is 100% grass-fed, our buns are baked fresh daily, and our sauces are all house-made from scratch.
